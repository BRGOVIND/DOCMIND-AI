Storage and privacy

Production-ready direction
  • Without keys, DocMind runs in local demo mode and keeps uploaded files inside the Docker container.
  • With Clerk keys, requests are tied to signed-in users and user workspaces stay separated.
  • With Supabase keys, compressed files are uploaded to your configured storage bucket.
  • PDFs are optimized before storage; images are resized and converted to WEBP.
  • The clear workspace action removes the active local index and local uploaded files for the current user.
